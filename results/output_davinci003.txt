Then there was Lira, a robot from the alien world of Tyron. Next was Alex, a human from Earth, and then there were two others, a rock creature from Zebron and an energy being from the planet of Xylon.

The Clever Five had formed a bond of friendship and were always eager to help each other and find solutions to any problem they encountered. Together, they had become known far and wide as a force to be reckoned with.

One day, the five friends were out exploring the star system when they came across a strange and powerful energy source. Little did they know that their cleverness and teamwork would be needed to help them unlock its secrets and protect the universe from its immense power.

The five friends worked together, using their intelligence and creativity to come up with a plan. After careful consideration, they decided to use their collective strength and special abilities to activate the energy source and contain the power within.

In the end, the Clever Five saved the universe and were hailed as heroes. From that day forward, the five friends were known as the Five Smart and Brave friends and they lived happily ever after.
